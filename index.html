Two budgets, no subscription

The WAVE software is a one-time purchase on the museum's own budget, a perpetual license with no recurring fees. The camera replacement is a separate purchase later, under the grant. That separation is the whole point: two budgets, no subscription, and the platform decision never gets handed to a lowest-bid camera procurement.

Assumptions & exclusions
Assumes
  • Your 60 existing cameras (about 15 outdoor, 45 indoor) onboarded to WAVE.
  • 4 to 6 week video retention, matching what you keep today. This sizes the server to about 80TB.
  • Existing Pelco cameras are ONVIF-compatible and the current cabling and network are adequate.
Excludes
  • PTZ control hardware. You confirmed all fixed dome cameras, so none is expected; added only if any PTZ turn up at the survey.
  • New cabling and network upgrades beyond what's in place.
